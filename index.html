Privacy Policy for MoodReset

Last Updated: November 29, 2025

Introduction

MoodReset ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Emotional State: The emotions you select (anxiety, stress, conflict, failure, low)
  • Context: Optional text or voice recordings you provide about your current situation
  • Preferences: Settings you configure (breathing duration, reminder times, voice preferences)

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: General app usage statistics (features used, session frequency)
  • Device Information: Device type, iOS version (collected anonymously)

Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Personal Identifiable Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information
  • Location Data: We do not collect or track your location
  • Contacts: We do not access your contacts
  • Photos: We do not access your photos or camera

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Generate personalized emotional support messages
  • Provide text-to-speech functionality for your messages
  • Save your sessions locally on your device for your reference
  • Improve the App's functionality and user experience
All data processing occurs locally on your device or through secure API calls to our backend service.

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All saved sessions are stored locally on your device only
  • Your data is never transmitted to our servers for storage
  • You can delete your data at any time through the App

API Calls

  • When you request a reset message, your emotion, tone, and context are sent to our backend API
  • These API calls are:
    • Encrypted using HTTPS
    • Protected by rate limiting
    • Not stored or logged on our servers
    • Processed and immediately discarded after generating your response

Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI: Used to generate personalized messages (your data is sent to OpenAI's API, subject to their privacy policy)
  • ElevenLabs: Used for text-to-speech functionality (your messages are sent to ElevenLabs' API, subject to their privacy policy)
  • Render: Our backend hosting service (processes requests but does not store your data)

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Your data is only shared with:

  1. OpenAI (for message generation) - subject to OpenAI's privacy policy
  2. ElevenLabs (for text-to-speech) - subject to ElevenLabs' privacy policy

These services are necessary for the App's core functionality.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored locally in the App
  • Delete: Remove any saved sessions at any time
  • Control: Choose what information to share (context is optional)
  • Uninstall: Remove all data by uninstalling the App

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • For significant changes, we may notify you through the App
